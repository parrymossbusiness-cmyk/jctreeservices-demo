Ask us directly
What services do you provide?+

JC Tree Services provides tree pruning, tree removal, dead tree removal, and stump grinding for homes in Little Rock, Arkansas.

How do I request a quote?+

Call or text (501) 240-3451. Describe the tree, where it sits on your property, and the work you think you need. Photos can help start the conversation.

Can you remove a dead tree?+

Yes. Dead tree removal is one of our core services. Because dead wood can be unpredictable, it is smart to discuss a declining tree before branches begin to fail.

Do you clean up after the work?+

Cleanup is one of the most common compliments in our customer reviews. Confirm the exact hauling, debris, and stump scope when discussing your job.

Why grind a stump after removal?+

Grinding can improve the look of the yard, remove a mowing obstacle, and make the area easier to reuse. Ask how stump grinding fits into your removal plan.
